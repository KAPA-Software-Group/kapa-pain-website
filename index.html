Our Philosophy
01

Chronic pain deserves more than a prescription. It deserves a plan.

We built Precision Pain Centre around a single conviction: every patient deserves specialist-led, evidence-based care — without financial barriers, without excessive wait times, and without compromise. Our multidisciplinary team works together to understand your pain from every angle and treat it with the precision it demands.

02 — Specialties

Every dimension of pain, addressed.

01 Image-Guided Procedures

Fluoroscopic and ultrasound-guided injections deliver treatment precisely to the source — with real-time imaging accuracy.

02 Regenerative & Sports Therapy

PRP, prolotherapy, and sport-specific rehabilitation that activate the body's natural healing potential for lasting recovery.

03 Medication Management

Pharmacological strategies that balance effective pain control with long-term safety, quality of life, and patient wellbeing.

04 Migraine & Headache Care

Neurologist-led diagnosis and ongoing management of chronic migraines, cluster headaches, and tension-type disorders.

05 Mental Health Integration

Psychological care woven into your treatment plan — because chronic pain and mental wellbeing are never separate.
